Hawking Radiation

Stephen Hawking's most famous idea is known as Hawking Radiation. The radiation can be observed as something that reduces the mass and the energy of a black hole and is also known as black hole evaporation. Black holes that lose more mass than they gain can be estimated to shrink and then vanish.

Singularity Theorems

Singularity describes the conditions at which gravitational singularities can form, similar to the theory of the Big Bang. A singularity is a point in which spacetime becomes infinitely dense. They are often associated with black holes, since the gravity is so strong, that nothing can escape from the event horizon.

Theoretical Cosmology

Hawking explored the concept of the Bing Bang's singularity, proposing that the universe originated from a point of singularity 13.8 billion years ago. This theory in particular helped set the stage for more information about the universe's expansion, including inflationary cosmology.
