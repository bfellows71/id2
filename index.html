Let's take a closer look into who Stephen Hawking was.

Stephen Hawking

Stephen Hawking was a theoretical physicist, cosmologist, and author. He was born on Jan 8, 1942 in Oxford, England. He passed away on March 14th, 2018. He made some crazy contributions to our understanding of the universe, specifically in the fields of cosmology, general relativity, and quantum mechanics. Unfortunately, he was diagnosed with ALS (amytrophic lateral sclerosis), which eventually paralyzed him, but that didn't stop his work. Some of his most notable achievements are:

  • Hawking Radiation
  • Singularity Theorems
  • Theoretical Cosmology
